All The POSCA Colors, Nib Sizes, and Hex/RGB Codes

Have you heard of the Posca marker? I have seen them all over social media and wanted to give them a try. I bought an 8 pack a few years ago, and then I saw a 16 pack. I figured by then I would have all the colors. I went to do a video about the markers and found out there are even more colors available. There are 65 Posca marker colors available in the United States. While I was searching I found out that some of the Posca markers colors in the UK are different color name United States. That was super confusing. I could only find Glacier Blue and it is on it's way here right now. The others I could not find, maybe they do not make them anymore?

This blog post contains affiliate links.

This blog post contains all the information for every size of Posca marker, the nib size and what colors they come in. I will also specify the color number for the marker. I found the Japanese markers do not have the number on the marker that I could read. I found most of the information on the Posca website, but I found it the website was outdated and found more information on what was available based on what was sold on websites.

Blick has a lot of the markers available as individual markers and in packs.

All the Posca Colors Sizes

There are 65 Posca marker colors available and I was able to get all the colors. It was about 200 to get every color. If I wanted to get all the colors in every size it would be 800-1000. I got most of my markers at Blick Art Supplies. There are some colors not available there and I got the rest from Jerry's Art Supplies and JoAnn Fabric Store. Michael's also has many colors and sizes available. That was one of the hardest things about finding all the colors. They were not available in one package and from the same place. It took a couple of months, but I got all the colors.

Here is a break down of all the colors available in each size for the United States. There are different colors available in different countries. I will have to figure out which down the road and update. I know the PC-1MR size had different colors because I ordered some from England and when I got they they were not the same as my list.

The PC-1M, 0.7mm Posca Pen is available in 22 colors.

  • Beige, Black, Blue, Bright Yellow, Brown, Gold, Green, Ivory, Light Blue, Light Green, Light Orange, Light Pink, Orange, Pink, Red, Red Wine, Silver, Slate Grey, Straw Yellow, Violet, White, Yellow


The PC-1MR, 0.7mm Posca Pen is available in 26 colors, but only 21 are available in the US and 22 are available in the UK. I have highlighted the ones only available in a certain country.
  • The 21 US colors are: Black, Blue, Gold, Green, Light Blue, Light Green, Light Orange, Light Pink, Metallic Blue, Metallic Green, Metallic Pink, Metallic Red, Orange, Pink, Red, Red Wine, Silver, Straw Yellow, Violet, White, Yellow
  • The 22 UK colors are: Apricot, Aqua Green, Black, Blue, Glacier Blue, Gold, Green, Lavender, Light Blue, Light Pink, Metallic Blue, Metallic Green, Metallic Pink, Metallic Red, Orange, Pink, Red, Silver, Sunshine Yellow, Violet, White, Yellow

The PC-5M, 1.8-2.5mm Posca Pen is available in 49 colors.

The PC-8K, 8mm Posca Pen is available in 35 colors.
  • Beige, Black, Blue, Bronze, Bright Yellow, Brown, Fluorescent Green, Fluorescent Light Orange, Fluorescent Orange, Fluorescent Pink, Fluorescent Red, Fluorescent Yellow, Gold, Green, Grey, Ivory, Light Blue, Light Green, Light Orange, Metallic Blue, Metallic Green, Metallic Pink, Metallic Red, Metallic Violet, Orange, Pink, Red, Red Wine, Silver, Slate Grey, Straw Yellow, Turquoise, Violet, White, Yellow

      POSCA Marker Colours & Their Pantone (PMS) Equivalents

      At PoscART, we understand how important colour consistency is for artists, designers, and creative professionals. Whether you’re working across print and digital mediums or developing artwork for commercial branding, ensuring your colours match is key to a flawless finish.

      This is why we’ve developed a practical and accurate guide that matches POSCA marker colours with their closest Pantone (PMS) equivalents, based on precise HEX sampling from actual marker tips.

      Why PMS Matching Matters

      The Pantone Matching System (PMS) is the global standard in print and design. Matching POSCA colours to Pantone values helps maintain accuracy and reliability across different formats—whether you're preparing artwork for printing, packaging, signage, or digital replication.

      How We Created the Reference

      We conducted detailed colour sampling using high-resolution scans of POSCA marker tips. These values were then converted into standard HEX and RGB colour codes and aligned to the nearest Pantone equivalents using professional comparison tools.

      This approach ensures the chart reflects actual marker output, not just theoretical colour codes—making it an ideal reference for artists and professionals who value precision.

      Who Should Use This?

      • Graphic Designers matching brand colours with physical POSCA work
      • Artists and Illustrators combining digital and hand-drawn media
      • Print professionals creating packaging or signage mock-ups
      • Teachers & Students exploring colour theory and consistency

      To request your copy, simply contact our team or email us directly. We’ll be happy to send you the format that best suits your needs.  Alternatively, download the Posca Colour Swatch here.

      Final Thoughts

      At PoscART, we’re committed to helping our creative community work smarter and more confidently. If you have a colour-matching request or need advice on the best marker for a project, we’re just a click away.
